Jordan

Executive Assessment

Jordan is classified as a Specialized Market within Version 2.0.

Although relatively modest in absolute market size, Jordan possesses a mature engineering community, an established ICT sector and a favorable environment for technology partnerships serving both domestic and regional markets.

Version 2.0 identifies Jordan as an attractive location for engineering cooperation, secure government infrastructure and specialized industrial computing deployments rather than large-scale volume commercialization.

⸻

Market Characteristics

Jordan has developed a diversified technology ecosystem supported by government digital transformation initiatives and a highly qualified engineering workforce.

Key market characteristics include:

* Mature software and engineering sector.
* Government digital transformation programs.
* Stable ICT ecosystem.
* Regional systems integration capabilities.
* Growing cybersecurity and secure infrastructure requirements.
* Strong educational and technical talent base.

These characteristics make Jordan particularly suitable for engineering collaboration and localized technology development.
